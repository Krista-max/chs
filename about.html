Excellence on Identity Catholic Standard School

It means an educational institution that demonstrates the characteristics of operations according to the Catholic education standards, the Education and Training Division, Catholic School Group under the Diocese of Chanthaburi (Catholic Standard School), which consists of standards in terms of learners, education management, learning society creation, identity and promotion measures. School History

Christ Songkhro School is located at 184 Moo 2, Rangsit - Nakhon Nayok Road, Ongkharak District, Nakhon Nayok Province, on an area of ​​14 rai. It is a Catholic school under the Diocese of Chanthaburi and is under the Private Education Commission.

2005 - 2006 Father Manop Preechawut acted as the license representative and Father Anusorn Phongsiriphat acted as the manager and passed the external quality assessment (second round) from the Office for National Education Standards and Quality Assessment (Public Organization). 2010-2014, Father Napha Kuchat acted as the representative of the license holder. In 2011, it was assessed and certified externally (third round) by the Office for National Education Standards and Quality Assessment (Public Organization).

At first, in 2558, Father Chiang organized a school for young children to read and write only. There were about 30 students. In 2469, Father Seni officially established the school and named it “Redemtor School”. In 2483, during Father Samut’s time, the name was changed to - Mahathai Public School. Later, in 2488, Father Charoen Phanitkasem requested permission to establish a private school from the Ministry of Education. It opened for teaching from Prathom 1 to Prathom 4 and was permitted to change the name to “Christian Welfare School” on May 2, 2489, with Mr. Samai Chinapha as the first headmaster. In 2502, during Father Bunnueng Worasit’s time, the school requested to expand the classes from Prathom 1 to Prathom 4 to Prathom 1 to Prathom 7.

In 2000 - 2004, the Diocese of Chanthaburi assigned Father Dr. Chatchai Phongsiri to be the administrator of the Christian Welfare School. Father carried out the construction of the multi-purpose building "St. Lawrence", a swimming pool, and improved many other buildings and facilities. He also led the school to undergo external quality assessment (first round) from the Office for National Education Standards and Quality Assessment (Public Organization).
